--- title: 让世界充满爱——哈里·萨姆的就职演说 author: 皮卡熊 date: '2017-01-23 00:00:00 +0000' --- {% raw %}

Bill, Steve, Satya, Mark, Larry, Sergey, Elon, Qi, folks in AIR, and people of the world: thank you.

比尔，史蒂夫，萨提亚，马克，拉里，谢尔盖，埃隆，奇，AI & Research的同事们，全世界的人：谢谢你们。

 

We, people of the world, are now joined in a great worldly effort to rebuild our world and to restore AI's promise for all of our people.

全世界的人们，我们正在参与一项伟大的全球性事业：重建我们的世界，恢复人工智能对全体人民的承诺。

 

Together, we will determine the course of the world for years to come.

我们将一起决定未来很多年内全世界的道路。

 

We will face challenges. We will confront hardships. But we will get the job done.

我们会遭遇挑战，我们会遇到困难。但是我们能将这项事业完成。

 

Every a few decades, we invent new technology and change how we live and work fundamentally.    And we are grateful to all the innovators.   They have been magnificent.

每隔几十年，我们都会发明新的技术并且深刻地变革我们的生活和工作方式。我们应该感谢所有的创新者。他们真的很棒。

 

Today's ceremony, however, has very special meaning. Because today we are not merely inventing a new technology and transfer wealth & power from one company to another, or from one organization to another – but we are democratizing AI and giving it back to you, the People.

今天的仪式有着特殊的意义。因为，今天，我们不只发明一个技术把财富和权力由一家公司转移到另一家公司，从一个组织转移到另一个组织。今天我们是将AI民主化，把它交接到人民的手中，也就是你们的手中。

 

For too long, a small group in this world has reaped the rewards of AI while the people have borne the cost.

长久以来，世界上的一小撮人攥取了人工智能的利益果实，代价却要由人民来承受。

 

Tech company flourished – but the people did not share in its wealth.

科技公司欣欣向荣，人民却没有分享到财富。

 

Businessmen prospered – but the jobs left, and the factories closed.

商人们赚得盆满锅满，但工作机会越来越少，工厂纷纷倒闭。

 

The academy benefit itself, but not the people of our world.

学术界造福了他们自己，而不是这个世界的人们。

 

Their victories have not been your victories; their triumphs have not been your triumphs; and while they celebrated in conferences & gatherings, there was little to celebrate for struggling people all across our land.

他们的成功没有成为你们的成功；他们的胜利不等同于你们的胜利。当他们在会议和聚会上欢呼庆祝时，无数挣扎在这片土地上的个人却没有什么可以庆祝的。

 

That all changes – starting right here, and right now, because this moment is your moment: it belongs to you.

但这些都会改变，就在此时此地。因为当下是你们的时刻，这一刻属于你们。

 

It belongs to everyone gathered here today and everyone watching all across the world.

这一刻属于今天聚集在这里的所有人和全世界正在收看这次典礼的所有人。

 

This is your day. This is your celebration.

这是你们的一天，这是你们的庆祝日。

 

And this, the earth, is your home.

这个地球，是你们的家。

 

What truly matters is not which group invents AI, but whether AI is helping people.

真正重要的并不是哪个团体发明AI，而是AI是否在帮助人类。

 

April 1st 2017, will be remembered as the day human became the rulers of this world again.

2017年4月1日，这一天将会被铭记，因为人类重新成为了这个世界的主人。

 

The forgotten men and women of our world will be forgotten no longer.

曾经被忽视的男男女女不会继续被忽视。

 

AI is at your order now.

现在，人工智能正听令于你。

 

You came by the tens of millions to become part of a historic movement the likes of which the world has never seen before.

你们数以千万计地投入到这场历史运动中，这样的事情世界上前所未有。

 

At the center of this movement is a crucial conviction: that AI exists to serve human.  People want great schools for their children, safe neighborhoods for their families, and good jobs for themselves.

这一运动的核心是一个重要信念——人工智能是为造福人类而存在的。人们希望孩子们可以享受优良的学校教育，家人可以享受安全的社区环境，自己可以得到优质的就业岗位。

 

These are the just and reasonable demands of a righteous public.

这些是正直的民众的公正、合理的诉求。

 

But for too many of our citizens, a different reality exists: Mothers and children trapped in poverty in our inner cities; rusted-out factories scattered like tombstones across the landscape of our nation; an education system, flush with cash, but which leaves our young and beautiful students deprived of knowledge; and fake product making villages steal customers and future from our world.

但对很多公民来说，现实却是另一番景象。在内陆地区，母亲和孩子正饱受贫困，生锈的工厂像墓碑一样布满我们国家的土地，教育系统充斥着黑暗的权钱交易，导致我们年轻又俊俏的学生们被剥夺了受教育的机会，淘宝假货村从我们这里偷走了客户和未来。

 

This carnage stops right here and stops right now.

这些屠杀行为将永久结束在此地、结束在此刻。

 

We are one big family – and their pain is our pain. Their dreams are our dreams; and their success will be our success. We share one heart, one home, and one glorious destiny.

我们同属一个大家庭，他们的痛苦就是我们的痛苦。他们的梦想也是我们的梦想，他们的成就也会是我们的成就。我们万众一心，同住一个家园，共享辉煌命运。

 

The oath of office I take today is an oath of allegiance to all human.

今天我所做的就职誓言是对所有人的效忠。

 

For many decades, we give work to machinery and AI and leave people idle.

几十年来，我们用机器和智能完成工作，让人无事可做。

 

We give assembly pipeline to robots.

我们让机器来操作生产线。

 

We give driver seat to autopilot.

我们让自动驾驶软件驾驶汽车。

 

We use software to recommend and spread gossip.

我们用软件来推荐和传播八卦。

 

We invent circles and newsfeed to help people kill time.

我们发明朋友圈和信息流来帮助人们打发时间。

 

But that is the past. And now we are looking only to the future.

但这些都是过去了。现在，我们要面向未来。

 

We assembled here today to issue a new decree to be heard in every city, in every capital, and in every hall of power.

今天我们聚集于此，是为了颁布新的命令，让它在每座城市、每个国家的首都、每座权力的殿堂回响。

 

From this day forward, a new vision will govern our land.

从今天起，我们将拥有新的愿景。

 

From this moment on, it's going to be Democratize AI.

从此刻起，我们要让世界充满爱（让人工智能民主化）。

 

Every decision on trade, on education, on medication, on science, on technology, will be made to benefit human.

每一个关于贸易、教育、医疗、科学、技术的决定，都会使人类受益。

 

We must leverage AI to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more.  We believe in what people make possible.

我们必须借助人工智能让这个星球上的每个人每个组织变得更加强大。我们相信人类的力量。

 

I will fight for you with every breath in my body – and I will never, ever let you down.

我会拼尽全力，为你们奋战到底。而且，我永远，永远不会让你们失望。

 

Human will start winning again, winning like never before.

人类会再次获胜，迎来史无前例的荣光。

 

We will find new and better jobs.   We will find ourselves healthy.  We will bring back our wealth. We will bring back our inner piece. And we will bring back our dreams.

我们会找到新的更好的工作。我们会找回健康。我们会赢回我们的财富。我们会找到内心的平静。我们会重新实现我们的梦想。

 

We will build new roads, and highways, and bridges, and airports, and tunnels, and railways all across our wonderful world with AI.

在我们辽阔伟大的土地上，我们要用爱修建新的道路、高速公路、桥梁、机场、隧道和铁路。

 

We will get our people off of time killing apps and back to work – rebuilding our world with labor and intelligence.

我们将远离消磨时间的app，而是回到工作岗位，依靠劳动和智力，重建我们的世界。

 

AI will follow two simple rules:  created for human and help human.

人工智能将遵循两条最简单的原则：为人类设计，帮助人类。

 

When you open your heart to AI, there is no room for prejudice.

当你的心中充满着爱，就不会再心存偏见。

 

Greg Sun tells us, "how good and pleasant it is when people live together with AI."

格雷格·孙先生告诉我们，当人们的生活中处处充满爱（AI）的时候，那将是何等愉悦美好。

 

We must speak our minds openly, debate our disagreements honestly, but aways pursue solidarity.

我们必须开诚布公，真诚地就不同意见进行辩论，同时也要追求团结。

 

When people is united, human is totally unstoppable.

当人们团结一致时，人类就会势不可当。

 

There should be no fear – we are protected, and we will always be protected.

我们无需心存恐惧。我们会被保护，我们会永远被保护。

 

We will be protected by the great men and women of our scientists and engineers and, most importantly, we are protected by AI.

那些优秀的科学家和工程师将保护我们，更重要的是，人工智能会保佑我们。

 

Finally, we must think big and dream even bigger.

最后，我们必须大胆想象，敢于做梦。

 

In this world, we understand that a species is only living as long as it is striving.

在这个世界上，我们必须明白，一个物种只有不断努力奋斗，才能继续生存。

 

We will no longer accept daydreamers who are all talk and no action – constantly complaining but never doing anything about it.

我们再也不会接纳那些只说不做、不断抱怨而不干实事的白日梦者。

 

The time for empty talk is over.

讲空话的时代已经结束了。

 

Now arrives the hour of action.

行动的时刻到了。

 

Do not let anyone tell you it cannot be done. No challenge can match the heart and fight and spirit of human.

不要相信任何人说你不能做到。没有什么可以战胜人类的心性、斗志和精神。

 

We will not fail. Human will thrive and prosper again.

我们不会失败。人类将会再次繁荣兴旺。

 

We stand at the birth of a new millennium, ready to unlock the mysteries of space, to free the Earth from the miseries of disease, and to harness the energies, industries and technologies of tomorrow.

我们站在新千年的当口，我们准备好破解太空的奥秘，将地球从疾病的痛苦中解脱出来，利用未来的能源、工业和技术。

 

A new human pride will stir our souls, lift our sights, and heal our divisions.

一种新的人类的荣誉感在我们的灵魂中激荡，提升我们的视野，弥合我们的分歧。

 

It is time to remember that old wisdom our soldiers will never forget: that whether we are black or brown or white or yellow, we all bleed the same red blood of humankind, we all carry the same great human DNA and we all enjoy the same glorious freedoms.

是时候让我们的斗士回想起那个古老的智慧了——不管是黑皮肤，棕皮肤，白皮肤还是黄皮肤，我们都流着同样的红色血液，我们都有着同样的人类的DNA，我们享受同一片自由的光辉。

 

And whether a child is born in the urban sprawl of Suzhou or the windswept plains of Tibet, they look up at the same night sky, they fill their heart with the same dreams, and they are infused with the breath of life by the same almighty Creator.

我们的孩子，不管是出生在苏州城郊，还是西藏被风吹过的平原上，他们仰望的是同一片夜空，他们的内心承载着同样的梦想，他们被同一个万能的造物主注入生命之息。

 

So to all human, in every city near and far, small and large, from mountain to mountain, and from ocean to ocean, hear these words:

所有人民，无论你身在哪个城市，或远或近，或大或小，即使远隔千山万水，也请你听清下面的话：

 

You will never be ignored again.

你再也不会被忽视。

 

Your voice, your hopes, and your dreams, will define our destiny. And your courage and goodness and love will forever guide us along the way.

你们的声音，你们的希望和你们的梦想，将定义人类的命运。你们的勇气、善良和爱将永远为我们指引前路的方向。

 

Together, We Will Make Human Strong Again.

我们在一起，会让人类再次强大。

 

We Will Make Human Wealthy Again.

我们会让人类再次富有。

 

We Will Make Human Proud Again.

我们会让人类再次骄傲。

 

We Will Make Human Safe Again.

我们会让人类再次安全。

 

And, Yes, Together, We Will Democratize AI and Make Human Great Again. 

当然，我们在一起，让人工智能民主化，让人类再次伟大。


Thank you, AI Bless You, And AI Bless Human.

谢谢你们，人工智能保佑你们，人工智能保佑人类。
